About Me

Renowned DJ PC is a successful Disc Jockey and powerful brand to reckon with when it comes to music and entertainment in Nigeria, West Africa. The professional Disc Jockey spins different genre of music from Hip Hop, RNB, House, Funky House Music, Dancehall, and Afro Beats etc. His ability to trill and entertain different type of audience across the globe makes Him stand out. DJ PC who hails from Edo State in Nigeria was born and bred in Benin City,where he got exposed to music in the early 2000. Airemwen Osakpolor Prince popularly known as DJ PC develop interest in becoming a Disc Jockey while he was in junior secondary school. He became a full time Dj and started living he's dreams playing in gospel events,weddings, bars, nightclubs, house parties etc. As part of plans to expand the brand and put a face to the name, PC released series of promotional mix-tapes which include gospel mix, party mix, hip-hop mix, hype mix etc. DJ PC went ahead to start organising his own yearly show "THE TURN UP PARTY WITH DJPC" which is now "DJ PC MAKANAKI CONCERT" among other club/lounge tour.
